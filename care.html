Jewellery Care

Keep your pieces shining for a lifetime

01. Wear

Avoid contact with liquids, perfumes, and lotions. Put your jewelry on last when getting ready.

02. Clean

Gently polish with a soft lint-free cloth after each use to remove skin oils.

03. Store

Keep your pieces in their original Auré pouch or a lined jewelry box in a cool, dry place.
