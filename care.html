Jewellery Care

Keep your icons shining

Wear with Care

Avoid contact with perfumes, hairsprays, and lotions. Always put your jewellery on last.

Cleaning

Gently wipe with a soft microfiber cloth after each wear to remove oils and dust.

Storage

Store in your Auré pouch or a cool, dry place to prevent scratching and oxidation.
