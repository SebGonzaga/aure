Behind Auré

The Visionary Heart

Raiza Tabudlong

Founder & Creative Director

"Each piece tells a story only the night can understand."

Auré was born from a desire to create jewellery that doesn't just decorate, but empowers. Based in Tanauan City, Batangas, Raiza oversees the journey of every icon—from the first sketch to the final polish. Her philosophy is simple: jewellery should be as bold as the person wearing it.

Purposeful Design

We don't follow trends. We create pieces with intention, designed to be icons of self-expression.

Crafted with Care

Every detail is scrutinized to ensure quality that lasts as long as your stories do.
